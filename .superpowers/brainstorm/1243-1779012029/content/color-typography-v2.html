Research notes shaping these:
· NN/G and UXmatters: tweens 10–12 reject primary-color "kid" palettes; they want to look mature.
· Color psychology: cool tones (blue/green) read as calming and focus-enhancing for educational contexts.
· Duolingo brand uses both bright green (#58CC02 / #22C55E) and dodger blue (#1CB0F6) — proven combo for kids+learning.
· 2026 trends: deep + warm-accent (Spruce), confident mono (Sprout), dark + vibrant accent (Midnight), editorial cream (Ember).
